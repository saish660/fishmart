Welcome to Amcho Pasro

Your marketplace for locally made products in Goa

At Amcho Pasro, we bring you a curated selection of authentic, locally made products from Goa’s creators and small businesses. We’re committed to quality, transparency, and attentive service—connecting you with great local finds as they become available.

Challenges in Finding Local Products

Quality Uncertainty

Shoppers struggle to discover authentic local goods and often miss out on unique products made nearby.

Limited Access

Many areas lack a reliable way to browse local sellers, leading to limited access and fewer options.

Price Transparency

Hidden markups and unclear pricing make it hard for customers to get fair value on local products.

Sustainability Concerns

People want to support local artisans and sustainable businesses but lack information about who makes the products and how.

Our Solution

Amcho Pasro bridges the gap between local makers and buyers with clear sourcing and pricing information. We connect you with trusted sellers across Goa and make it easy to discover new products as they come in.
